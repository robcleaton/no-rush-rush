Rules

PlayPlay a card equal to or higher than the top pile card.
2 — WildPlays on anything, resets the pile. Play again.
3 — ChainPlays on anything. Next player must play a 3 or pick up the pile. Chains until someone can't.
8 — WindowPlays on anything. Next player plays on the card below the 8 (or anything if pile is empty below).
7 — LowPlays on anything except a 3. Next player must play 7 or lower.
10 — BurnPlays on anything. Burn the pile. Play again.
Four of a kindFour of the same value on top of the pile burns it. Play again.

Play from Hand first, then Face-up, then Face-down (blind flip). If you can't play, pick up the pile. First player to empty all cards wins.
